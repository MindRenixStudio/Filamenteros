# Filamenteros
For keep up to date with you fillament remaining spool
# What is this all about
That's pretty simple. You print, and print and print an then, you have 1/8 remaining filament on your spool and you don't know how much you have. Sad. Solution is simple, just use Filamenteros,
it's simple app. You hit print on your 3D printer and fill a few columns here and app keep track of your filament, 3D printer and models.
